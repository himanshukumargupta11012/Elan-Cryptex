CRYPTEX 2023
Cryptex is one of India's largest online treasure hunts conducted by Elan & nVision, IIT Hyderabad's annual techno-cultural fest.

It contains a series of questions with diverse difficulties and formats that will keep you hooked for 54 hours.
HOW TO PLAY
There are no pre-requisites required.
As any puzzle you would need to think through all the elements given in it, and we mean all. All you need is a good search engine and a working mind to connect the results.
And trust us, you will be ecstatic when you make them!

Cryptex collaborated with India In Pixels(IIP) for an India-based Online Treasure Hunt for the IIP community named 'IndiaQuest'. India in Pixels is a data storytelling channel that makes video essays that explores connections in the cultural artefacts of India - languages, films, music, stories, and life. The event was a grand success with prize money of 10K and a participant count of several hundreds.
